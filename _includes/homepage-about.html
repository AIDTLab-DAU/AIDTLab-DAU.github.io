About Lab

Welcome to AI Digital Twin Lab at Dong-A University. Our lab is dedicated to cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, with a strong emphasis on deep learning, computer vision, and digital twin technologies. Our work addresses both theoretical advancements and practical applications, aiming to bridge the gap between academic research and real-world challenges.

We actively explore emerging trends in AI and foster an interdisciplinary environment that encourages collaboration among students, researchers, and industry partners. Supported by advanced computing infrastructure and guided by experienced faculty, the lab strives to push the boundaries of innovation in AI-driven modeling, simulation, and intelligent systems.
